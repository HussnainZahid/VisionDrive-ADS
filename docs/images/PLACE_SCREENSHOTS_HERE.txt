Quick way to extract from your output recording:
  python -c "
  import cv2
  cap = cv2.VideoCapture('data/output_recording.mp4')
  cap.set(cv2.CAP_PROP_POS_FRAMES, 300)
  ok, frame = cap.read()
  if ok:
      cv2.imwrite('docs/images/hud_dashboard.png', frame)
  cap.release()
  "
